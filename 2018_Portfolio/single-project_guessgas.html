GuessGas

GuessGas is the driving friendly APP. We provide the users the simplest way for the cheap gas, just one click. GuessGas reduces the effort of searching the gas station and guides driver to the closest and cheaper gas station.

Understand User

We observed that people have problems for searching the suitable gas stations when they are driving. They didn't know where they can find a gas station. They also concern the gas prices and the risk of driving far. We want to solve this question!

Through the online questionnaire and the interview with target users. The users tell us:


  • I care about the safety, the operation of the APP should be quick and easy. I need to focus on the road.
  • I need to focus on the road, so the information of the navigation should be clear and easy to understand.
  • I have some favor brands. I prefer to go to those gas stations.
  • I need to know how much I spent on the gas.

Design

1. Provide the accurate information for the users. Connect with the most powerful resource, google (google map and the gas price)

2. Permanent service, GuessGas replace the "google now" and stand by for users' requirement.


3. Safety = Simplicity

Reduce the time spent on operating the APP, just 1 click, GuessGas will start the navigation. Drivers need to focus on traffic thus we eliminate the operation steps. Users don't have to spend time on selection and decision making.


4. According to the users' habit and preference records, GuessGas will automatically match the suitable gas station for them.
